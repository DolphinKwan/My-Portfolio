Pick a day! As mentioned in Chapter 2, the time for the Moon to go through all its phases is about 29.5 days Which is different from the number of days we have on our calendar. So, the date of the full moon keeps changing each month. However, thanks to the Internet, we can easily google the date of the full moon on the Internet. Tips If you are not available or the weather is not permitting on the day of Full Moon, you can still check out the day right after the full Moon. Since the moonrise time will only be later every day, the Moon will also rise after sunset, and it will still be about the same size as the day of full Moon.
