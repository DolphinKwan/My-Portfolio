Research/Tangible
03/30/2019 - 06/14/2019
Arduino
Technique: Storyboarding
Role: Researcher,Tangible interface designer
Project Description

When walking with a stroller in downtown Chicago, a parent needs to keep a high level of concentration on the babies' safety and, at the same time, let them enjoy the sun and the air. However, the stroller will highly confront the parent's movement and their route. In the observation, parent's behaviors were affected by strollers. Here are some insights.

Design Process

When walking with a stroller in downtown Chicago, a parent needs to keep a high level of concentration on the babies' safety and, at the same time

Observations
My research strategy is diving right into the observation. I followed people walking around the street and interact with the city. I observed how they performed all kinds of tasks. I found that I was only looking at the surface of their actions. I didn't understand what's in their mind when they are just walking on the street. So even I did a considerable amount of observation, I'm still confused about the intention of their moves. Due to the massive gap between the study population and me, I decided to conduct several informal interviews with my friends to picture better the target subjects and the tasks they perform. It showed me the journey of going out with a baby.
