What is Moonrise?

It’s a precious treasure for Chicago people to have Lake Michigan on the east. We can always observe the most significant two objects in the sky—The Sun and The Moon, rising from the horizon. Unlike the Sun, which we have already gotten used to its track, the motion and shape of the Moon seem more unpredictable. It makes most of us don’t have the chance to observe this spectacular scene of moonrise.
In the Moonrise E-learning system, I will teach you the mechanism behind the Full Moonrise and the hardcore astronomy knowledge involved so that you can observe yourself!

Learning Objectives


  • Recalling(1.2) the four main moon phases and their moon rise time (Ab. Knowledge of specific details and elements)
  • Explaining(2.7) the cause of Moon phase (Ab. Knowledge of specific details and elements.)
  • Explaining(2.7) how we see moonrise and moonset (Ab. Knowledge of specific details and elements.)
  • Explaining(2.7) the mechanism behind this scene. (Bc. Knowledge of theories, models, and structures)
  • Executing(3.1) the preparation of watching full moonrise. (Cc. Knowledge of criteria for determining when to use appropriate procedures)

Pre-test

This pre-test is to test your knowledge regarding the motion of the Moon. Comparing the pre-test and post-test scores, we can evaluate the efficiency of the system and keep improving it. Please finish the pre-test before you start the learning journey!
